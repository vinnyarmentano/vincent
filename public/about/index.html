About

My name is Vincent Armentano and I am currently a PhD student at the Department of Economics at UC San Diego. I hold a BA in Economics from Northeastern University. My main interests lie in development economics and applied microeconomics with a special focus on agricultural production. Currently I am working on projects concerning smallholder farmers’ expectations of rainfall and risk averse behavior as well as examining potential labor misallocation and tenuous property rights.
